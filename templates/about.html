About NayePankh Foundation

Empowering communities through volunteer-driven initiatives, awareness campaigns, and social impact programs.

Who We Are

NayePankh Foundation was established during the COVID-19 pandemic with the goal of supporting communities during challenging times. What started as a small initiative has grown into a youth-led organization dedicated to uplifting underprivileged sections of society.

We work across multiple areas including food distribution, clothing donations, menstrual hygiene awareness, educational support, and community outreach programs.
